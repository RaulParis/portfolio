Objects Detection - Car License Plates Detection & Recognition using YOLOv8

In this project, I show an example of application of the state-of-the-art (SOTA) model in computer vision, YOLOv8. It is a very easy example of how to build a car license plates recogniser. This kind of applications can be used for various purposes, such as traffic monitoring, parking management, security surveillance, and toll collection. By leveraging the power of YOLOv8, this car license plate recognition system provides fast and accurate detection of plates, even in challenging conditions such as low light, complex backgrounds, and varying distances.
